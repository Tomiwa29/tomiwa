About Us

SPUDS is Tomiwa's & Anu's continued love affair with food and drink! Classic bistro ideas and Southern ingredients are combined in a new space with an open kitchen and a full bar & raw bar. Since opening in 2005, Easy has evolved continually, and this most recent evolution has culminated in its relocation and re-imagining into a new space in the West Village. At Easy, we're constantly pushing ourselves to find new ways to redefine the dining experience and we are wholeheartedly embracing small shareable plates and large shareable meats, with house-made pasta and all the oysters and raw bar ingredients we can find

OurMenu

Our menu is meant to be shared...and create a family style dining experience. We curate experiences based on how we like to dine and have let go of the pre-conceptions of coursed dining. We’re not looking back.

In an effort to pioneer a new style of dining for Chattanoogans, the menu we’ve created is elemental at its core, mirroring the way you’d entertain guests in your home. Take the opportunity to try a whole slew of menu items with friends or family. We are pretty good at curating the nearly endless options for dish combinations with menu items that honor simplicity, yet never lack in creativity and flavor. We’re pushing the boundaries, and are so excited to have you in the restaurant.
